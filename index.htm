Guidelines for submissions

1.Abstracts should not exceed 400 words. They should contain the title, names of lead authors and co-authors, designation and place of work along with a valid email address

2.All abstracts should be submitted in MS Word format

3.The abstracts may be on any topic related to innovations in medicine, surgery and nursing care, management or could be an original piece of research, case study, description of a new procedure

4.The abstract should make the objective of the study clear. Methodology could include study design, duration, interventions and outcome measures. The conclusion should be precise and well supported directly by the data. Any financial gain or conflict of interest should be declared

The scientific committee will select the Abstracts for both oral or poster presentations and the lead authors will be notified.
